## Fulfillment Options

### Option A: Email-Based Delivery (Simplest)
- Use ConvertKit / Mailchimp / ActiveCampaign
- Trigger automated sequence on Stripe purchase (via webhook)
- Attach video modules as links (host on Vimeo/YouTube unlisted)
- Attach templates as downloadable files (Dropbox/Google Drive)

### Option B: Members Area (More Professional)
- Build simple members area using:
  - Teachable ($39/month)
  - Kajabi ($149/month)
  - Memberspace ($25/month)
  - Custom page with Vimeo embeds (DIY)
- Stripe webhook creates user account
- Customer gets instant login email

### Option C: Hybrid (Recommended for Launch)
- Week 1-4: Email delivery (fast to set up)
- Month 2+: Migrate to members area (scale-friendly)
