## Paid Ads Plan (Recommended Budget: $500-2,000/month)

### Platform Priority

1. **YouTube Ads** (Primary) — $300-1,000/month
   - Target: Video creators, podcasters
   - Ad format: In-stream video ads (60-90 seconds)
   - Targeting: Competitor channels (Descript, CapCut tutorials, editing courses)
   - Targeting: Keywords ("video editing tutorial," "how to edit faster")
   - Goal: $20-40 CPM, 2-5% CTR, $30-60 CAC

2. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** (Secondary) — $200-500/month
   - Target: YouTubers, content creators, coaches
   - Ad format: Video ads (short-form hooks)
   - Placement: Feed + Stories + Reels
   - Goal: $15-30 CAC

3. **X/Twitter Ads** (Optional) — $100-300/month
   - Target: Tech-savvy creators
   - Ad format: Promoted tweets with video
   - Goal: Brand awareness + retargeting

---

### Ad Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Cold Traffic (Top of Funnel)**
- Audience: Broad targeting (video creators, content creators, YouTubers)
- Ad creative: Long-form ad #1 or #2
- Landing page: Sales page
- Budget: 60% of total ($300-1,200/month)

**Campaign 2: Warm Traffic (Retargeting)**
- Audience: Website visitors, video viewers (50%+ watch time)
- Ad creative: Retargeting ad #1 or #2
- Landing page: Sales page (with urgency)
- Budget: 30% of total ($150-600/month)

**Campaign 3: Lookalike (Scaling)**
- Audience: Lookalike of purchasers (once you have 20+ sales)
- Ad creative: Best-performing ad from Campaign 1
- Landing page: Sales page
- Budget: 10% of total ($50-200/month)

# Stage 7 — Optimization + Scale Plan

## Optimization Levers (Month 2-3)

### Lever 1: Sales Page Conversion

**Test:**
- VSL vs. No VSL
- $297 vs. $397 vs. $197 (price testing)
- Guarantee placement (above vs. below fold)
- CTA button copy ("Enroll Now" vs. "Get Instant Access" vs. "Start Learning")

**Optimize for:** 2-5% conversion rate minimum

---

### Lever 2: Ad Creative

**Test:**
- Short-form vs. long-form ads
- Different hooks (pain vs. benefit vs. curiosity)
- Testimonials vs. demo vs. talking head
- Thumbnail variations

**Optimize for:** Lowest CAC (customer acquisition cost)

---

### Lever 3: Email Sequence

**Test:**
- 5-day vs. 7-day vs. 10-day sequence
- Subject line variations
- Story-driven vs. logic-driven emails
- Urgency timing (day 5 vs. day 7)

**Optimize for:** 10-20% email-to-sale conversion

---
